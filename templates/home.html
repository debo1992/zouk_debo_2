Why Dance With Us?

✨ Meet Matthew Chen ✨

Matthew Chen is a renowned Brazilian Zouk instructor celebrated for his precision, expressive artistry, and deep connection to the essence of the dance. With years of experience teaching and performing across the Asia–Pacific, Matthew brings a rare blend of clarity, energy, and elegance to every class.

Whether you’re stepping onto the floor for the first time or refining advanced technique, Matthew’s teaching nurtures confidence, musicality, and authentic partner connection. His unique approach combines strong foundations with artistic freedom—empowering every dancer to discover their own voice through movement.
