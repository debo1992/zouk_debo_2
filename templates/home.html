Why Dance?

✨ Meet Deboshree Bose ✨


Dance for Deboshree is a way to explore rhythm, connection, and expression across different styles. She's drawn to hip-hop for its groove and individuality, Bollywood for its energy and storytelling, and Brazilian Zouk for its fluidity and focus on partner connection.

Her background in swimming has shaped a strong sense of discipline, body awareness, and control, which she brings into her dance training. She's currently focused on building stronger foundations, improving musicality, and developing more ease and clarity in movement across styles.

She approaches dance as an ongoing practice: balancing technical improvement with creativity and curiosity. Whether learning choreography or social dancing, she's interested in growth, collaboration, and becoming more expressive and intentional in her movement.
