About Matthew Chen

Matthew Chen is a standout Brazilian Zouk instructor known for his precision, emotional expression, and deep connection to the dance. With years of experience training dancers across the Asia-Pacific region, Matthew brings clarity, energy, and elegance to every class he teaches.

Whether you're just starting out or looking to refine your advanced technique, Matthew guides dancers to build confidence, musicality, and meaningful partner connection. His teaching blends foundational structure with artistic flair, empowering students to find their own voice through movement.
